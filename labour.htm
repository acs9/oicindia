Gainful Employment / Labour Unrest Investigations
When it comes to Gainful Employment Investigations that involve conducting investigations in those cases, where an ex-employee files a case in the Labour Court against the Employer for being "unjustifiably" terminated, we at OIC have the expertise in successfully handling such cases. OIC interacts with the Human Resources and Legal department of the Client and after obtaining an insight into the case conducts investigations that focus on establishing with documentary proof the subsequent employment of the subject. Not only this, we also conduct audio-video recordings which can be presented in a Court of law as credible evidence.

We are a global detective agency, known for offering the best and specialized services related to Labour Cases. We have a exhaustive list of satisfied clients who gets benefitted with our services. With our professionally trained and highly experienced detectives, we make sure that the truth wins over lie. In cases related to nascent union activities or during severe union unrest, OIC with its team of experienced investigators monitors the activities of the 'key players' within and outside the unit to identify the 'unseen hand' behind all the labour unrest. With our in-depth knowledge and vast experience, we make sure to give you the best investigation services for a lifetime.
