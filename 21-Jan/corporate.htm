Surveillance Operations
Be it the corporate sector or an individual in society, the need to conduct surveillance or shadowing operation arises in both scenarios.

When it comes to the Corporate Sector, surveillance is a key service in corporate investigations of fraud, corruption, theft, truancy, wilful sickness and much more. There is no denying the fact that in today's society the corporate professionals as well as the businesses cannot afford to be lax. Keeping this in mind OIC provides to the corporate sectors as well as private individuals, bespoke Surveillance Solutions to challenge any situation.



We provide the service of covert surveillance carried out at the behest of the Management in order to monitor the activities of employees who may be of doubtful integrity or indulging in anti-management activities. Corporates harrased by frivilous litigation from unknown quarters do get a reprive on hiring our services to understand the dynamics behind the scene. Such surveillance operations are intended to obtain credible evidence to support the suspicions or prove the case otherwise.

Our highly experienced and specialized team of investigators along with well-equipped, state-of-the-art audio-visual recording systems and GPS Tracking Services successfully complete surveillance operations for many corporate clients around the world. Apart from this, our skillful and experienced investigators collect incriminating evidence by using practical techniques and current technology that provides you the best in class service.

Typical corporate investigations that involve surveillance operations are:

  • Fraud Investigations
  • Gainful Employment Investigations
  • Theft of Confidential Information
  • Substance Abuse at Workplace
  • Sexual Harassment Investigations
  • Labour Unrest Investigations

Our covert surveillance methods and techniques can be moulded to any requirement, hence we can give our valuable clients the exact type of service they need. By positioning a team of experienced investigators on a surveillance operation, OIC provides to its Client a detailed report that highlights:

  • The movements of the subject
  • The places visited by the subject
  • The persons met with details of the third party
  • The duration of the meeting
  • The nature of visits
  • Corroborating with photographic evidence where plausible
