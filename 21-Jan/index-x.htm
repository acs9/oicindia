Private Detective Agency in Delhi

Optimum Interface Consulting (OIC) is a provider of investigative and risk mitigation services. OIC is a premier investigation company in India, offering specialized services to Corporate sector as well as Private Individual. From investigation of illegal activities to sourcing information and providing security cover to one and all, we offer complete investigative solutions to a wide range of customers spread across diverse geographies. We provide a whole range of services such as GPS tracking, IPR investigations & enforcement actions, surveillance operations, fraud investigations, crime scene & forgery investigations including forensics, technical surveillance counter measures (TSCM), due diligence, asset verification, pre-employment verification (vetting), claim verifications, tracing absconding debtors, gainful employment investigations, pre-employment screening, pre matrimonial checks and post matrimonial investigations for divorce, maintenance/alimony and child custody, issues in addition to security audits, executive protection services and security systems.

With a track record of numerous successful cases, OIC is known for offering professional investigation services with an assured guarantee of 100% confidentiality. We have covered a broad spectrum of almost all the industry sectors but not limited to, the pharmaceutical, electrical, IT, automotive, finance, export houses, travel & hospitality, banking sector, education, chemicals, tobacco, FMCG, telecom, Export houses, reality and computer consumable industry. Intelligence Network Agency with its team of experienced investigators provides the best investigative services in Delhi, NCR in terms of a professional approach with solutions dove-tailed to suit each Client's needs by adopting ethical trade practices.
